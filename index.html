A New Way to See The News

Search beyond your filter bubble.

NewsIt scans news headlines and returns the most positive, negative, politicized and neutral perspectives on any search query.

Features

Comprehensive Search

Built on NewsAPI, NewsIt gathers and analyzes more than 30,000 global sources of news.

Comprehensive Reading

NewsIt A.I. underpins a singular diverse and easy environment to search and view news.

Comprehensive Intelligence

Using IBM Watson Natural Language units, NewsIT reads text from thousands of related articles in minutes, so you don't have to. Our research returning the most extreme endpoints of coverage about a topic.

Technologies

Built on the MERN Stack, using technologies from IBM Watson, NewsAPI and others.
